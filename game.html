How To Play The Game?

Join us for a thrilling murder mystery game set during the party. As guests mingle and enjoy holiday cheer, a crime has been committed. It's up to you and your fellow players to gather clues, question each other, and solve the mystery before the culprit gets away. Can you unravel the clues and catch the killer in time?

Rules

  • There are 5 suspects who left 5 unique clues (Check the clues table)
  • The real suspects themselves don't know they are suspects. They can still compete to find the other 4 suspects
  • You can only send the names of maximum 5 suspects


Check the evidence table

Head to the evidence table and inspect the items and tools the criminals have left behind. These will help you find the criminals. Read in between the lines

Start your investigation

Every guest with a green bracelet is a suspect. Your role is to start asking guests about their in-game profile characteristics and then evaluate whether they have committed the crime.
